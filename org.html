OHIO ORGANIZATIONS

SEED Ohio

 SEED Ohio is a group of Ohio based nonprofits committed to protecting our planet. They have organizations dedicated to land, water, climate, food, pollution, and wildlife. One donation to SEED provides resources to organizations in all of these categories. SEED Ohio is also affiliated with the global association 1% for the Planet which represents individuals, nonprofits, and businesses that are fighting pressing environmental issues.

Carbon Neutral Ohio

 Carbon Neutral Ohio (CNO) is a social enterprise startup focusing on helping households reduce their carbon footprints and shifting the norm to be carbon neutral. CNO helps individuals to measure their carbon footprint and then helps them reduce their direct emissions. They also collect a monthly donation from patrons in order to support carbon offset programs.

NATIONAL ORGANIZATIONS

Climate Neutral

 Carbon Neutral is a national nonprofit that promotes carbon neutrality amongst businesses. By providing resources for companies, they aid businesses in reducing their carbon footprint and allow consumers to have piece of mind that they are not contributing to excess greenhouse gases. To learn more about Climate Neutral and the companies their certified companies visit their website.

Carbon Fund

 Carbon Fund is nonprofit organization that works with carbon offset programs to counteract greenhouse gas emissions via monetary donations. They work with projects regarding renewable energy, energy efficiency, and forestry. Visit the Carbon Fund website to make a donation and offset your carbon footprint.
