What is a
Carbon Footprint?

A carbon footprint is the total amount of greenhouse gases emitted from an individual or group. Greenhouse gases include methane, nitrous oxide, water vapor, and most prominently, carbon dioxide. Greenhouse gases cause the sun’s heat to get trapped inside the earth’s atmosphere, causing the global temperature to rise.

Our
Goal

Our goal is to educate the community about greenhouse gas emissions and provide resources for individuals reduce their own carbon footprint. Minimizing carbon footprint is a global effort but it starts with one step.
