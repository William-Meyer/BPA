OUR MISSION

Our mission is to educate the community about their carbon footprint and achieve net zero carbon emmisions on a local and global scale.




Learn About Carbon Footprint


Greenhouse Gases

Greenhouse gases are gases that trap heat within the earth's atmosphere. With an increase in man-made greenhouse gases the layer of gases in the atmosphere has grown, therefore, raising global temperatures.

Carbon Footprint

Carbon footprint is the total amount of greenhouse gases generated by human actions. Carbon footprint is a great way to measure your personal greenhouse gas emissions.

The Impact

The largest contributor to climate change is the emission of greenhouse gases. Scientists are already seeing detrimental changes to our environment due to climate change.
