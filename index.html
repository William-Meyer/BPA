What is a Carbon Footprint?

A carbon footprint is the total amount of greenhouse gases emitted from an individual or group. Greenhouse gases include methane, nitrous oxide, water vapor, and most prominently, carbon dioxide. Greenhouse gases cause the sun’s heat to get trapped inside of the earth’s atmosphere, causing the global temperature to rise.


Our Goal

Our goal is to educate the community about greenhouse gas emissions, and provide resources for individuals to reduce their carbon footprints. The damage done to the earth is nearing an irreversible state, but with everyone's help, change can be made. Reducing the carbon footprint is a global effort, but all it takes is a little bit of change from each individual to make a huge difference.
